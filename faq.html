How can I register for the library? +

You can register for the library by visiting the front desk or signing up online through our website.

What are the library opening hours? +

The library is open from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM from Monday to Saturday. Closed on Sundays.

How many books can I borrow at a time? +

Students can borrow up to 5 books at a time for a period of 14 days.

How can I renew my borrowed books? +

You can renew your borrowed books online via your library account or by visiting the library desk.

What happens if I return a book late? +

Late returns will incur a fine of $0.50 per day per book. After 30 days, the book will be considered lost.

Can I reserve a book online? +

Yes, you can reserve books online through your library account. You will receive a notification when it's available.

Does the library offer e-books? +

Yes! The library provides access to a collection of e-books and digital resources through our online portal.
